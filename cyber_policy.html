I put this list together during March/April 2021 to help write a paper using public-facing search engines, but The IT Law Wiki deserves extra praise for providing a majority of my starting points. The reason I want to put it up is because I have not seen another collection of this type of information. After a few different suggestions from people (thank you!), I have expanded the timeline from legislature to "offical" governance and regulation. It is still far from perfect! A big thank you to those who have already, but feel free to suggest any corrections if I have gotten something wrong and I will do my best to fix the error. I am still only a casual observer of the cyber-legal space.
